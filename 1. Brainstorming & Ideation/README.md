# Phase 1: Brainstorming & Ideation

This folder contains the ideation, problem definition, and customer empathy mappings for **Personalized Networking Assistant**.

---

## 1. Problem Statement

Students, professionals, and job seekers often find it difficult to build meaningful professional connections. Existing networking platforms provide thousands of profiles but offer limited guidance on initiating conversations, discovering relevant networking events, or receiving personalized recommendations based on individual interests and career goals. As a result, users spend significant time searching for opportunities and often miss valuable professional connections.

**Goal**: Develop an AI-powered Personalized Networking Assistant that helps users discover relevant networking opportunities, generate personalized conversation topics, analyze networking events, fact-check shared information, and maintain interaction history to support meaningful professional relationships.

---

## 2. Empathy Map

This map represents the thoughts, feelings, and actions of a typical target user: **A Final-Year Engineering Student** preparing for internships, placements, and professional networking opportunities.

| What the User... | Deliverable Details / Observations |
| :--- | :--- |
| **THINKS & FEELS** | * "I want to connect with professionals, but I don't know how to start conversations."<br>* Feels nervous while approaching recruiters or mentors.<br>* Wants personalized networking suggestions instead of random recommendations.<br>* Wishes there was an easier way to prepare for networking events. |
| **HEARS** | * Advice from seniors about the importance of networking.<br>* Placement officers encouraging students to build professional connections.<br>* Recruiters emphasizing communication and networking skills.<br>* Success stories from students who secured opportunities through networking. |
| **SAYS & DOES** | * Creates professional profiles on networking platforms.<br>* Searches for mentors, recruiters, and alumni.<br>* Attends career fairs, webinars, and technical events.<br>* Tries to maintain professional conversations and build long-term connections. |
| **SEES** | * Large numbers of professional profiles with little personalization.<br>* Multiple networking platforms requiring manual searches.<br>* Career events and opportunities shared across different platforms.<br>* Professionals discussing industry trends and opportunities online. |
| **PAINS (Challenges)** | * Difficulty finding relevant people to connect with.<br>* Lack of confidence in starting conversations.<br>* Time-consuming manual searches for networking opportunities.<br>* Limited personalized guidance during networking activities. |
| **GAINS (Desired Outcomes)** | * Personalized networking recommendations.<br>* AI-assisted conversation topic suggestions.<br>* Better preparation for networking events.<br>* Stronger professional relationships and career opportunities. |
