# Phase 1: Brainstorming & Ideation

This folder contains the ideation, problem definition, and customer empathy mappings for **Personalized Networking Assistant – AI-Powered Professional Networking Platform**.

---

## 1. Problem Statement

Students and young professionals often struggle to build meaningful professional connections despite having access to networking platforms. They spend significant time searching for relevant mentors, professionals, and networking events while lacking guidance on how to initiate conversations or identify opportunities aligned with their interests and career goals. Existing platforms primarily focus on connecting users but provide limited personalized assistance for effective networking.

**Goal**: Develop an intelligent AI-powered networking assistant that helps users discover relevant networking opportunities, generate personalized conversation topics, analyze networking events, verify shared information, and maintain interaction history to build stronger professional relationships.

---

## 2. Empathy Map

This map represents the thoughts, feelings, and actions of a typical target user: **A Final-Year Engineering Student** preparing for internships, placements, hackathons, and professional networking opportunities.

| What the User... | Deliverable Details / Observations |
| :--- | :--- |
| **THINKS & FEELS** | * "I want to connect with professionals but don't know how to start the conversation."<br>* Feels anxious while approaching recruiters or experienced professionals.<br>* Wants personalized networking suggestions instead of random recommendations.<br>* Wishes networking was easier and more organized. |
| **HEARS** | * Seniors advising that networking is important for placements and internships.<br>* Recruiters encouraging students to build strong professional profiles.<br>* Mentors emphasizing communication and relationship building.<br>* Success stories of students receiving opportunities through networking. |
| **SAYS & DOES** | * Creates professional profiles on networking platforms.<br>* Searches for mentors, alumni, recruiters, and industry experts.<br>* Participates in technical events, webinars, and hackathons.<br>* Tries to maintain professional conversations and expand their network. |
| **SEES** | * Thousands of professional profiles with little personalization.<br>* Numerous networking events shared across different platforms.<br>* Professionals discussing career opportunities and industry trends.<br>* Multiple platforms requiring users to manually search for relevant connections. |
| **PAINS (Challenges)** | * Difficulty identifying the right people to connect with.<br>* Lack of confidence in initiating conversations.<br>* Time-consuming manual search for networking opportunities.<br>* Limited personalized assistance while networking. |
| **GAINS (Desired Outcomes)** | * AI-powered networking recommendations tailored to personal interests.<br>* Personalized conversation topic suggestions.<br>* Better preparation for networking events and professional interactions.<br>* Stronger professional relationships leading to internships and career opportunities. |
